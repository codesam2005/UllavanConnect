Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are needed to apply for PM-KISAN?

You'll need Aadhaar card, land ownership documents, bank account details, and a self-declaration form. For joint holdings, all owners need to submit documents.

How long does it take to get Kisan Credit Card approval?

Typically 15-30 days after submitting a complete application at your nearest bank branch. The timeline may vary based on document verification.

Can tenant farmers apply for these schemes?

Yes, many schemes like PMFBY and KCC are available to tenant farmers. You'll need to provide tenant farming agreement documents.

Is there any helpline for scheme-related queries?

Yes, you can call the Kisan Call Center at 1800-180-1551 or contact your district agriculture officer for assistance.

How can I check my PM-KISAN payment status?

Visit the PM-KISAN portal (pmkisan.gov.in) and use your Aadhaar number, mobile number, or bank account number to check status.
